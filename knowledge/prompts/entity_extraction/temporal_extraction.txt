## DATE EXTRACTION PATTERNS

### Absolute Date Formats

**Standard Date Formats:**
- ISO Format: 2024-01-15, 2024/01/15
- US Format: 01/15/2024, 1/15/24, 01-15-2024
- European Format: 15/01/2024, 15.01.2024, 15-01-2024
- Written Format: January 15, 2024; Jan 15, 2024; 15 Jan 2024

**Date with Day of Week:**
- "Monday, January 15, 2024"
- "Mon Jan 15"
- "Monday the 15th"
- "15th of January"

**Short Date References:**
- Today: 2024-01-15 (if today is 2024-01-15)
- Tomorrow: 2024-01-16
- Yesterday: 2024-01-14
- This/Next/Last + Day: "This Monday", "Next Friday", "Last Tuesday"

### Relative Date Patterns

**Week-based References:**
- This week: Current Monday through Sunday
- Next week: Following Monday through Sunday  
- Last week: Previous Monday through Sunday
- Week of [date]: Week containing specified date
- In 2 weeks: Two weeks from current date

**Month-based References:**
- This month: Current calendar month
- Next month: Following calendar month
- Last month: Previous calendar month
- End of month: Last day of current month
- Beginning of month: First day of current month

**Business Day References:**
# UPDATE: Customize based on your business calendar
- Next business day: Next Monday-Friday (excluding holidays)
- 2 business days: Two weekdays from now
- End of week: This Friday
- Beginning of week: This Monday

### Fuzzy Date References

**Temporal Proximity:**
- Soon: Within 3 days
- Later this week: Thursday-Friday if today is Monday-Wednesday
- Early next week: Monday-Tuesday of next week
- Mid-month: Days 10-20 of current month
- Quarter end: Last month of current quarter

**Seasonal References:**
- This quarter: Current 3-month business quarter
- Next quarter: Following 3-month period
- End of year: December (last month of fiscal/calendar year)
- Beginning of year: January (first month of year)

## TIME EXTRACTION PATTERNS

### Standard Time Formats

**12-Hour Format:**
- 9:00 AM, 9 AM, 9:00am, 9am
- 2:30 PM, 2:30pm, 2:30 P.M.
- 12:00 PM (noon), 12:00 AM (midnight)
- Half-hour increments: 9:30, 2:15, 11:45

**24-Hour Format:**
- 09:00, 14:30, 23:45
- 0900 hours, 1430 hours (military time)
- 9h00, 14h30 (international format)

**Casual Time References:**
- Morning: 6:00 AM - 11:59 AM
- Afternoon: 12:00 PM - 5:59 PM  
- Evening: 6:00 PM - 9:59 PM
- Night: 10:00 PM - 5:59 AM

### Business Time Patterns

**Standard Business Hours:**
- Business hours: 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM (Monday-Friday)
- Office hours: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM
- Extended hours: 7:00 AM - 8:00 PM
- Weekend hours: 9:00 AM - 3:00 PM (if applicable)

**Shift-based References:**

- First shift: 7:00 AM - 3:00 PM
- Second shift: 3:00 PM - 11:00 PM
- Third shift: 11:00 PM - 7:00 AM
- Day shift: 6:00 AM - 6:00 PM
- Night shift: 6:00 PM - 6:00 AM

**Meeting Time Conventions:**
- Start of day: 8:00 AM - 9:00 AM
- Mid-morning: 9:30 AM - 11:00 AM
- Lunch time: 12:00 PM - 1:00 PM
- Mid-afternoon: 2:00 PM - 4:00 PM
- End of day: 4:00 PM - 6:00 PM

### Time Range Extraction

**Explicit Ranges:**
- "From 9 AM to 5 PM"
- "Between 2:00 and 4:30"
- "9:00 - 11:30"
- "Starting at 10 AM until 2 PM"

**Implicit Ranges:**
- "Morning meeting" → 9:00 AM - 11:00 AM
- "Lunch appointment" → 12:00 PM - 1:00 PM
- "Afternoon service" → 1:00 PM - 5:00 PM
- "All day" → Business hours (8:00 AM - 6:00 PM)

**Duration-based Ranges:**
- "For 2 hours starting at 9 AM" → 9:00 AM - 11:00 AM
- "30-minute appointment at 2 PM" → 2:00 PM - 2:30 PM
- "Quick 15-minute service" → Duration: 15 minutes

### Priority and Urgency Indicators

**Urgency Levels:**
- Emergency/ASAP: Within 1 hour
- Urgent: Same day
- High priority: Within 24 hours
- Normal priority: Within business week
- Low priority: When convenient

**Emergency Time Patterns:**
- "As soon as possible" → ASAP flag, earliest available slot
- "Immediately" → Emergency flag, interrupt current schedule
- "First available" → Next open appointment slot
- "At your earliest convenience" → Normal priority scheduling

## VALIDATION AND CONFIDENCE SCORING

### High Confidence Patterns (0.9-1.0)
- Explicit date and time: "January 15, 2024 at 2:30 PM"
- Standard formats: "2024-01-15 14:30"
- Clear business context: "Next Tuesday at 9 AM"
- Validated against calendar: Confirmed valid date

### Medium Confidence Patterns (0.7-0.89)
- Relative dates: "Next Monday"  
- Casual times: "Morning", "Afternoon"
- Implicit ranges: "Lunch meeting"
- Ambiguous formats: "01/02/2024" (could be Jan 2 or Feb 1)

### Low Confidence Patterns (0.5-0.69)
- Very casual: "Later", "Soon"
- Ambiguous references: "Next week sometime"
- Missing components: Date without time or vice versa
- Conflicting information: "Monday the 15th" when 15th isn't Monday

### Validation Rules

**Date Validation:**
- Must be valid calendar date
- Cannot be in distant past (older than 1 year typically)
- Cannot be too far in future (beyond 2 years typically)
- Must account for leap years
- Verify day-of-week matches if both specified

**Time Validation:**
- Valid 24-hour range: 00:00-23:59
- Valid 12-hour range with AM/PM
- Business hour constraints for routine operations
- Emergency time allowances for urgent requests

**Cross-Validation:**
- Date/time combination must be logical
- Must not conflict with known blackout periods
- Should align with business operational hours
- Consider time zone consistency
